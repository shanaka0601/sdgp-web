Quick Read Prescription Scanning System

Enhancing accuracy, efficiency, and safety in prescription handling

Project Overview

One of the major challenges in the healthcare industry is medication errors caused by unclear handwritten prescriptions. This system utilizes OCR and NLP technologies to accurately read, interpret, and transmit prescriptions digitally — reducing human errors and improving communication between healthcare professionals and pharmacies.

Project Scope

✅ In-Scope Features

  • OCR + NLP Prescription Scanning
  • User Interface for Uploading & Viewing
  • Secure Digital Prescription Transfer
  • Record Storage & Data Management
  • Error Detection for Unclear Text
  • Improved Accuracy, Efficiency & Safety

❌ Out-of-Scope Items

  • Diagnosis or Drug Prescribing
  • Payment or Insurance Processing
  • Real-Time Consultations
  • Drug Interaction Checks
  • Full EHR Integration
  • Clinical Decision Making
